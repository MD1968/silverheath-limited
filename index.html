About us

Silverheath was formed in 2019 to provide a quality Full Stack Design service. We aim to provide quality and personal service to anyone who is looking to gain or enhance their presence on the Internet.

Every website created by us has been fully researched to enable our team to understand the client’s requirements. Wireframes and marks-ups are then created, and the sites design is agreed after full consultation with the client.

Coding takes place whilst keeping the client updated, including uploading the code onto our test server. This enables everyone to be able to keep track of the process. Once everyone is happy, we then publish the code onto a live site.
